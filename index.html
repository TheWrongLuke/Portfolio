ProfitHub is a website my classmate and I designed and developed as part of a school project in just four days. We created a concept for a platform that could generate revenue if fully developed.

Our idea is a game-based earning website, where both players and the company can profit. With the gaming industry continuing to grow, many gamers would love the opportunity to earn money simply by playing their favorite games.

Revenue Model

  • Banner Ads: We plan to monetize the platform through banner advertisements on the website.
  • Optional Subscription: In the future, we could offer an optional subscription service through a dedicated app that tracks gameplay, offering subscribers exclusive benefits and rewards.
  • Sponsorships: Collaborating with content creators and providing discount codes to their audiences could bring more users to our platform, enhancing visibility and engagement.
